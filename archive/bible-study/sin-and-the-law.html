Bible Study - Sin and the Law

  1. Who is the originator of sin? 1 John 3:8
  2. What is sin? 1 John 3:4, Rom. 3:23, Phil 3:14
  3. What is the purpose of the law? Rom. 7:7, Rom.3:20
  4. Were sin and the law present in Eden? Gen. 2:16, 17, 3:1-6
    1. What are some of the universally applicable consequences of sin? Gen. 3:21, 23
    2. How does sin affect our relationship with God? Gen 3: 8-10 Isa 59:2
    3. What opens our eyes to sin? Gen 3:7-8, John 16:7-8, Additional: Mark 1:3-4, Luke 1:7, Mark 9:13, 2 Kings 6:17, Psalm 146:8, Luke 4:18,
    4. How does the Holy Spirit convict us of sin? Gen. 3:11, Tim. 3:16,17, Ps. 119:11
  5. Does the law pertain to us today? Isa. 40:8, Matt. 5:18
  6. Will it always be smooth sailing for keepers of the law? Revelation 12:17, Rev 14:12
  7. What is promised to keepers of the law? Ps. 119:165, Ps. 19:7-9, Rev. 22:14, 1-5

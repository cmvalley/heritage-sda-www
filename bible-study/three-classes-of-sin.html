Bible Study - The Three Classes of Sin

  1. What is sin? Rom. 3:23, Phil 3:14, 1 John 3:4
  2. What are the three classes of sin? 1 John 2:16
    1. What is the lust of the flesh? Gal. 5:17-21, 1 Thess. 4:3-5, 1 Cor. 6:13, Proverbs 6:25, 2 Tim. 2:16-17,22, 1 Pet. 2:1,11-12
    2. How does sin affect our relationship with God? Gen 3: 8-10 Isa 59:2
    3. What is the lust of the eyes? Psalm 101:3, Matt 6:22, Exodus 20:17, Job 31:1. Futher Reading: 2 Samuel 11
    4. What is the pride of life? Luke 12:16-21
  3. How do we know that these three classes of sin are all encompassing? Heb. 4:15
  4. Case Study – Jesus and the three classes of sin Matt. 3:16-17, 4:1-11
    1. Why did Jesus go into the wilderness? Matt. 4:1
    2. Why would the Spirit lead Jesus into the wilderness to be tempted? Matt. 6:13, James 1:13, 1 Cor. 10:13, Exodus 13:17-18
    3. What was Jesus doing while in the wilderness? Matt 4:2, Acts 9:9, Acts 13:1-3 Further Reading Exodus 15:22 - 16:36
    4. Can we see the tempter luring Jesus into each category of sin? Matt. 4:3-11, John 16:7-8
    5. Can we see him doing the same in the Garden of Eden? Gen. 3:1-6
    6. What can we learn about overcoming temptation from Jesus’ preparation and response to temptation? Matt. 4:2, Mark 14:38, Isa. 58:6-7, Matt. 4:3-11, Psalm 119:11, John 17:17 Further Reading: Deut. 8:2-3, Deut 6:13, Deut. 6:17, Exodus 17:7
  5. What is promised to us if we resist the devil in all areas of sin? James 1:12, Rev. 21:7

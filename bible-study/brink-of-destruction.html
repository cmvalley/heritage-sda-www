Bible Study - Back Away from The Ledge

  1. What is the destruction of man? 2 Thessalonians 1:8-9, Revelation 21:8
  2. How do we get to the brink of destruction? Matthew 7:13, Proverbs 16:18, Proverbs 14:12, Romans 8:6, Galations 6:8, Philippians 3:18-19, 2 Timothy 3:2-5, Mark 3:28-29
  3. Can we be at the brink of destruction and not know it? Matthew 27:38-39, Luke 17:28-29, Matthew 15:10-14
  4. What are some lessons we can learn from Biblical examples of those that have separated themselves from God?
    1. Saul. 1 Samuel 15:10-11,13, 16:14
    2. Hophni & Phinehas. 1 Samuel 2:12-17, 25
    3. Achan. Joshua 6:17-19
    4. Ananias & Sapphira. Acts 5:1-4,9, Isaiah 29:15, Matthew 4:6,7
    5. Israel. Exodus 17:3,7, Deuteronomy 6:16
    6. Religious Leaders. Matthew 16:1, Matthew 12:22-24, Acts 7:54-58
  5. How can we recognize if we/others are at the brink of destruction? Matthew 24:12, Revelation 3:14-18, Luke 6:43-45, Galatians 5:19-20
  6. How can we return or help others return from the brink of destruction? Psalm 95:8-10, Ezekiel 33:11, Isaiah 1:16-17, Hebrews 3:13
  7. What are some lessons we can learn with people that returned from the brink of destruction
    1. Solomon. Ecclesiastes 1:1-2, Ecclesiastes 12:13
    2. David. 2 Samuel 12:13, 2 Samuel 24:10
  8. How can we avoid getting to the brink of destruction? Matthew 7:12, John 15:12, Matthew 22:37-40, Galatians 6:8, 1 Timothy 6:8-10, Matthew 6:19-21 , 1 Thessalonians 5:12-22, 2 Peter 2:1-3

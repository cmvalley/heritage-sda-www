Bible Studies

Bible Study - Holy Habits Fasting

  1. What is the earliest recorded fast in the Bible? Deut. 9:9,18-19, Ex. 34:28
  2. What are some of the reasons for fasting in the bible?
    1. Mourning: 1 Samuel 31:13, 2 Samuel 1:12, Judges 20:26
    2. Asking for Deliverance: Dan. 9:3,20, Neh. 1:4
    3. Sorrow for Sin: Neh. 9:1, Lev. 16:31
  3. What does God say about fasting? Leviticus 16:27-31, Joel 2:12-13, 1 Kings 21:17, Isaiah 58:6-7, Matt 6:16-18, Matt. 9:14-15
  4. Did Jesus fast? Matt. 4:2, Luke 4:2
  5. Case Study on Moses vs Jesus' fast Matt. 4:2, Luke 4:2
  6. What are some similarities between Moses and Jesus' fast? Exodus 19:9, Matt. 3:17
  7. What is a connecting theme between Jesus' fast and Moses fast? Exodus 34:28, Daniel 9:27, Jeremiah 31:37, 31-33, John 4:21-24, John 5:25
  8. How should we fast today? Matt 6:16-18, Phil. 4:6, Gen. 32:26 (Gen. 28:13-16), Acts 9:9-19 Further reading Acts 13:1-3, Acts 14:21-23

Bible Study - Don't Look Back

  1. Who are some people saved from desperate situations through the mercy of God? John 8:2-11, Leviticus 20:10, Gen 19:12-26, John 5:5-8
  2. What was God’s instruction to each of them? John 8:11, Gen 19:17, John 5:14
  3. What do we call someone in today’s society that keeps doing the same thing over and over again, expecting different results?
  4. What’s the alternative that the Bible calls them? Prov 26:11
  5. So what are we doing when we keep returning to the sinful lives we have been saved from? Ps 14:1, Mark 7:13
  6. What eventually happens to us if we keep returning to the things we’ve been saved from? 2 Pet 2:20-22, Luke 11:24-26
  7. How do we avoid returning to the things we have been saved from?
    1. Trust His Word. Isa 55:11, 1 Cor 10:13
    2. Change your friends/habits. Ps 1:1-2, Luke 14:26, Luke 9:24
    3. Ask for directions. Prov 14:12, Prov 3:6, John 14:6, Ps 119:105, Matt 7:13-14, John 10:9, Matt 7:7
    4. Keep our eyes fixed on Jesus. John 8:12, Matt 14:29-30, Num 21:9, John 12:32, 2 Cor 3:18
